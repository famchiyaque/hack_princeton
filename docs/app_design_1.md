Implementation Prompts: Kinetic AI Fitness App

Use these prompts with your code editor agent to build the high-fidelity views for the Kinetic app. Each prompt specifies the layout, styling (using Tailwind CSS), and core functionality based on the designs we've created.



1. Welcome / Entry Screen

Prompt:



Create a high-fidelity mobile landing page for an AI fitness app named "Kinetic". 
Layout: Use a full-height background image of a focused athlete in a gym with a dark overlay. 
Typography: Place a large, bold "KINETIC" logo at the top center with the tagline "INTELLIGENCE IN MOTION". 
Content: In the center, feature the headline "Evolve Your Human Potential" in a mix of white and primary orange (#FF7A00). Below that, include two translucent "glassmorphism" cards side-by-side showing stats: "99.8% Accuracy" and "AI Driven Insights". 
Actions: A large, rounded primary orange "Get Started" button at the bottom, followed by a "Sign In" link for existing users.
Style: Modern, premium, and energetic.



2. Onboarding: Fitness Goals

Prompt:



Design an onboarding step for a fitness app where users select their primary goal. 
Header: Show a progress bar at the top labeled "STEP 01 / 04". Large headline: "What is your primary fitness goal?".
Goal Selection: A 2x2 grid of large, rounded cards. Each card should have a unique icon (e.g., dumbbell for "Build Muscle", heart for "Lose Weight") and a short description. 
State: Show one card (e.g., "Improve Form") with a primary orange border and a checkmark to indicate it's selected.
Actions: A prominent "Continue" button at the bottom with a right-arrow icon.
Style: Clean white background, soft shadows, and Manrope typography.



3. Home Dashboard

Prompt:



Create a mobile dashboard for the Kinetic app. 
Header: User profile avatar on the left, "KINETIC" logo in the center, and a settings gear icon on the right. 
Greeting: A personalized "Welcome back, Alex!" with a "5-DAY STREAK" badge in a soft orange pill.
Stats & Program: Two small cards for "Hydration" and "Calories" above a large, dark image card for "Today's Program: Legs & Glutes".
Primary CTA: A large, centered orange button labeled "Start Session" with a play icon.
Insights: A "Weekly Insights" section at the bottom with list items for "Strength Recovery" and "Sleep Quality" showing trend icons.
Navigation: A fixed bottom navigation bar with icons for Home, Workouts, Insights, and Profile.



4. Live Session - Camera Interface

Prompt:



Build a camera-view interface for a real-time form correction session.
Background: A live camera feed (use a placeholder image of a gym).
Overlays: 





A top bar with a "04:42" timer and a "FORM: GOOD" status indicator.



A human skeletal outline (AR-style) overlaid on the center of the frame to guide positioning.



Floating "glass" widgets on the right showing Heart Rate (BPM), Calories (KCAL), and Reps.



A bottom-left text bubble with AI coaching advice: "Keep your chest up...".
Controls: Large red "Stop" button at the bottom center, flanked by "Pause" and "Flip Camera" buttons.

5. Session Report

Prompt:



Design a post-workout session report screen.
Header: "Mastery Achieved" headline with a "Session Complete!" badge.
Key Stats: Large cards at the top for "Duration: 45m", "Squats: 30 reps", and a "Form Score: 85%" with a progress bar.
Form Insights: A vertical list of cards with icons. Example cards: "Great depth on squats" (success/check) and "Watch Out: Heel Lift" (warning/alert).
Visual Feedback: A section titled "Form Precision" showing a rep-by-rep graph or breakdown.
Actions: A primary "Done" button and a secondary "Share Results" button at the bottom.
Navigation: Keep the bottom nav bar visible.
